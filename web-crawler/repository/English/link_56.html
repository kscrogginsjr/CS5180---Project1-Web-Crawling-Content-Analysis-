2004–2007

A mix of meeting notes, agendas, and summary minutes

2007

  1. very short board meeting during Wikimania, Taipei, Taiwan, August 2007 (no notes)
  2. Advisory board meeting, Taipei, Taiwan, August 2007
  3. Board meeting, Amsterdam, Netherlands, June 2007 (summary only)
  4. Board meeting, Tampa, Florida, March 2007 (summary only)
  5. Board Meeting, Rotterdam, Netherlands, January 2007 (summary only)

2006

  1. Board Meeting, November, 2006, St. Petersburg, Florida
  2. Board Meeting, October, 2006, Frankfurt, Germany (general summary only, by mail Election of board officers, Statement of new chair)
  3. Board Retreat, October 2006, Frankfurt, Germany (Outcome)
  4. Board Meeting at Wikimania August 2006, Cambridge, MA (no notes)
    • Board Panel Discussion at Wikimania, a practice continued in future years
  5. Board meeting, January 14-15, 2006, St. Petersburg, Florida (no notes? resulting notes were sent by email about Committees and other structural ideas)
  6. Two unofficial open meetings were also held in early January, a practice resumed in 2009.
  • Note: Following the 2006 Board Retreat, the Executive Director and Chair started a weekly IRC meeting attended by most/all Board members, that ran from November through March of 2007. It also recorded minutes.
